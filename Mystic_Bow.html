Mystic Bows are mystic items obtained extremely rarely from killing players while the killer has the Mysticism renown upgrade.

While unenchanted, Mystic Bows are functionally identical to normal bows. However, they can be upgraded in the Mystic Well. Upgrading to tier I costs 1,000g, upgrading to tier II costs 4,000g, and upgrading to tier III costs 8,000g, requires Mysticism IV, and requires a sacrifice of a randomly-chosen color of Mystic Pants.

Enchanting Mystic Bows gives them a random number of lives that increases on every tier and removes their Kept on Death status. Lives decrease by 1 on death, or by 3 if the user logs out while in combat. Depleting all lives on a mystic item "breaks" it, destroying it forever.

When enchanted, Mystic Bows gain random enchantments with varying effects. Upon upgrading to tier I, they gain 1–2 "tokens" – total tiers – of a low-grade enchantment. When upgrading to tier II, they gain 1–2 more tokens of any enchantment (at most one of which can be RARE!), and at tier III, they gain 1–4 more tokens (at most three of which can be RARE!) – up to 8 total.

Mystic Bows may not have more than 3 unique enchants or more than 8 tokens of enchantment, even through usage of the Totally Legit Gem.

Enchanted Mystic Bows also have certain prefixes if their properties match certain criteria.

  • Legendary: 8 total tokens
  • Extraordinary: 2 unique rare enchantments
  • Artifact: 100 max lives
  • Bountiful: 3 unique resource enchantments
  • Overpowered: At least 7 tokens and 100 lives
  • Miraculous: 2 unique rare enchantments and 100 lives
  • Unthinkable: 3 unique rare enchantments (impossible without admin interference)
  • One in a Million: 3 unique rare enchantments and 100 lives (impossible without admin interference)
