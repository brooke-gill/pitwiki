The Pit 1.0.1
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

The Pit 1.0.1 was a minor version of the Hypixel Pit. It introduced some balance changes and bug fixes, primarily relating to the prior update.

Balance changes:

  • (↑) Hermit reward boost now increases every (20 → 10) kills.
  • (↑) Hero's Haste killstreak's Speed II duration increased from ((0:04) → (0:05)).
  • (↑) Arquebusier killstreak's arrows increased from (12 → 16).
  • (↑) Fight or Flight killstreak's damage bonus increased from (+15% → +20%).
  • (↑) Khanate killstreak's stacking damage bonus increased from (+2% → +4%).
  • (↑) Bounty Solvent price reduced from (1,500g → 750g).
  • (↓) Beastmode's stacking defensive debuff now increases every (10 → 5) kills.
  • (↓) To the Moon's on-death XP bonus decreased from (0.01×XP → 0.005×XP) per kill. The cap remains the same.
  • (↓) Executioner enchantment threshold decreased at tiers I and II.
    • I: When hitting a player to under (1.5♥︎ → 1♥︎), deal (1.5♥︎ → 1♥︎) of true damage to them.
    • II: When hitting a player to under (2♥︎ → 1.5♥︎), deal (2♥︎ → 1.5♥︎) of true damage to them.
    • III: When hitting a player to under 2♥︎, deal 2♥︎ of true damage to them.
  • (↓) Assist Soups Regeneration duration decreased from ((0:07) → (0:05)).
  • (↓) Combat Spade price increased from (350g → 750g).
  • (↓) First-Aid Egg cooldown increased from (10 → 20) seconds.
  • (↓) Devil Chicks enchantment is disabled during major events.

Bug fixes:

  • Fixed not losing mystic lives when having Promotion!! unlocked without Assistant to the Streaker equipped.
  • Fixed cases where items would lose their Totally Legit Gem tag.
  • Fixed the Highlander megastreak not giving Speed I.
  • Fixed the Electrolytes enchantment not halving speed duration boost for Speed II+.
  • Fixed an issue where the last beasts in the Beast event do not receive Beast armor
  • Bounties are now properly cleared after leaving the game while in-combat with the Highlander megastreak active.
  • Scam Artist renown upgrade now applies to killstreaks.
  • Fixed Gold Nano-factory giving Regen II instead of Regen IV.
  • Nicked players' real names are no longer revealed by /view.
  • Assist Soups now count towards the healing item limit.
  • Players can no longer /trade spectators.
  • Melons from the Blockhead event no longer drop Sugar Extract when mined with an axe and do not respawn.
  • Fixed typo in the /oof command.
