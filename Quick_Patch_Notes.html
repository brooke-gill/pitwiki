Quick Patch Notes
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Quick Patch Notes was an intermediary version of The Pit, created between The Pit 0.1 and The Pit 0.2.

Changes:

Features
  • The game will now remember the player's preferred hotbar slots.
Balance
  • Nerfed Strength-Chaining multiple times.
  • Bounties no longer increase past 5,000 gold.
  • Added the Bounty Hunter perk.
  • Added a cooldown between eating Golden Heads.
  • Removed diamond pants from the shop.
  • Increased the prices of diamond chestplate and boots, to 250 gold and 150 gold respectively (previous prices were 200 gold and 100 gold).
  • Lava or blocks can no longer be placed in the center of The Pit.
  • Lowered price for the triggered confirmation menu[unclear] from 5,000 gold to 1,000 gold.
  • Extra picked up iron armor will attempt to go into the inventory instead of the player's hotbar.
  • Players can no longer drop their iron sword and bow.
  • Loosened collusion detection, causing Reduced kills to appear less often.
Bug Fixes
  • Fixed gold ingots not spawning.
  • Specified that only the leaderboards, not the game, get reset weekly.
  • The Pit now gives Hype, the Prototype Lobby's currency.
  • Fixed a cause for profiles rolling back.
  • Players now automatically join a new Pit instance when the old one reboots.
  • Fixed a bug where placing blocks on lava already in the map would remove the lava forever.
  • Fixed the obsidian shop item not giving 8 obsidian.
