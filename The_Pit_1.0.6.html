The Pit 0.2
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

The Pit 1.0.6 is the latest patch of the Hypixel Pit. It introduced the Bonk! perk, new ways to obtain Chunks of Vile, balance changes, bug fixes, and other minor changes.

The Insurance perk's name was changed to Bonk!. Instead of reviving the user, it now protects the user from the first hit from all players and gives them Resistance I (0:02) when blocking hits.

Some methods of obtaining Chunks of Vile were buffed.

  • The Do your Own Carpentry renown upgrade has been added to the Renown Shop. It increases the chance for Chunks of Vile to appear as rewards in contracts, and increases the vile reward to 2 for contracts that have them.
  • The Apostle to RNGesus killstreak's vile reward has been increased from 1 to 3. In game references to the reward still denote the reward as 1 vile.
  • Auctions can now have 3-6 Chunks of Vile as rewards.

Balance changes:

  • (↑) The Yummy renown upgrade now gives +6g instead of +3g when consuming golden apples and Golden Heads.
  • (↓) The Regularity mystic now reduces the damage of the second strike after additive bonuses are applied, rather than before.
  • (↓) The Trash Panda mystic now cannot be used while the Magnum Opus megastreak is equipped.
  • (↓) Bedrock can no longer be stored in enderchests.
  • (↓) Peroxide cooldown increased from 1 second to 1.5 seconds.
  • (↓) Dark Pants starting lives changed from a random multiple of 10 between 10 and 40 (inclusive) to a random multiple of 5 between 5 and 20 (inclusive).

Bug fixes:

  • Fixed usage of the Totally Legit Gem not properly doubling the vile cost for Magnum Opus.
  • Iron Pack can no longer be purchased for 0g when the player is already wearing all pieces of iron armor.
  • Fixed the Big Time achievement being unobtainable for players who did not purchase the Golden Pickaxe (Efficiency VII) when it was available in the Renown Shop.
  • Fixed giving gold when it was intended to give XP.
  • First-Aid Egg cooldown now resets on death.

Some exploits were also fixed:

  • A time check (estimated to be about 9 minutes) has been added to earning participation XP in order to prevent players from earning it more often than intended.
  • A distance limit (estimated to be about 4 blocks) has been added to the Ender Chest and NPCs, preventing players from using blacklisted modifications to access them remotely.
  • Players' streak is now reset when changing perks, preventing players from changing perks mid-streak following major events.

Two changes were additionally made to the Pit Supporter package:

  • Now obtainable at prestige I instead of II.
  • Can now add enchantment glint to the Fancy Hat. Glint has no gameplay effect.
