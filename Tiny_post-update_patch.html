Tiny post-update patch
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

The Tiny post-update patch was a minor version of the Hypixel Pit. It was an intermediary update between The Pit 0.2 and The Pit 0.3.

Changes:

  • The Spammer perk now gives +1.5 gold on all assists.
  • The Trickle-down perk now gives 7× gold when picking up ingots.
  • Changed the price of the Golden Heads perk to 500 gold.
  • The player's bounty now appears on their sidebar.
  • Kill recaps now show the killer's and victim's killstreak.
  • Changed the tab list to now sort by level (highest-to-lowest) rather than by prestige.
  • Ensured nicked prestiges are "obfuscated amongst the masses."
  • Added the missing sound effect when the player purchases diamond swords in the item shop.
  • Fixed infinite lava bug.
  • Reworded the El Gato passive.
