Rage Pants
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Rage Pants are special types of Mystic Pants. They can be crafted for 17 Chunks of Vile in the Heresy menu if the user has the Prestige IX renown upgrade Pure Rage.

Rage Pants function differently from standard Mystic Pants. When enchanted to tier I, they will always have 4 or 9 lives and 1–2 tiers of a special Rage enchant. Once enchanted to tier I, Rage Pants become functionally identical to Tier I standard Mystic Pants.

Trivia

On release, a RARE! rage enchant called Deal with the Devil was also released. It disabled the Devil Chicks enchant in an entire lobby and increased damage in Rage Pit. The enchantment was made unobtainable almost immediately.
