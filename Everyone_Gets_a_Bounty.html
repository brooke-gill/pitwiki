Everyone Gets a Bounty
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Everyone Gets a Bounty, commonly shortened to "All Bounty," is a minor event in the Hypixel Pit.

The event is instantaneous. When it occurs, every non-bountied player receives a bounty of 100 gold. If a player already has a bounty, their bounty increases by 100. There is no limit on bounties affected by the All Bounty event—due to this event, bounties have been seen as high as 99,000 gold.

Minikloon summoning twenty consecutive Everyone Gets a Bounty events.

Trivia

Everyone Gets a Bounty events can be triggered by commands from admins. Some admins enjoy repeatedly starting this event in lobbies.
