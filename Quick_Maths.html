Quick Maths
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Quick Maths is a minor event that occasionally occurs in The Pit. When the event starts, a randomly-generated math equation will appear in the chat. The first five players to answer it correctly will receive 250 XP and 500 gold. The equations are intended to be easy and solvable without a calculator, typically involving one- or two-digit addition and multiplication. Most Quick Maths events are over in under five seconds.

Trivia

  • Answering any Quick Maths problem in under 2.5 seconds for the first time will award the player with the "Genius Tier" achievement, worth 5 achievement points.
  • The name "Quick Maths" is likely a reference to the lyrics in the song "Man's Not Hot" by Big Shaq. The song, a viral hit at the time of The Pit's release, contains the lyrics "...Two plus two is four; minus one, that's three; quick maths."
