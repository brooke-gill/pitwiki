XP
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

XP (short for Experience) is an essential resource in The Pit. Similarly to gold, accumulating it is required to prestige. A player's XP determines their level and prestige. Players can view the XP they need to level up in the sidebar and their lifetime XP at the Stats NPC.

The following are a variety of ways players may obtain XP:

  • Killing players rewards a base of 5 XP.
  • Completing personal bonus tasks in certain major events rewards XP.
  • Eating chocolate chips in the Giant Cake give 100 XP.
  • Being one of the first 5 players to answer a Quick Maths question grants 250 XP.
  • Clicking the dragon egg rewards increasing amounts of XP per click.
  • Every 10 minutes, all players in the lobby receive "Participation XP," 1/6595th of the player's prestige requirement or 10,000 XP, whichever is smaller. It can be increased by the XP Boost passive upgrade.
  • 100 XP can be found as an item in sewer treasures and care packages.
  • Completing the King's Quest gives 30% of the player's XP requirement to prestige.
  • Potions from the Olympus perk grant 27 XP on consumption.
  • Night Quests grant 200 XP plus 25 XP per prestige the player has.
  • Killstreaks such as Second Gapple, Explicious, Ice Cube, and Apostle to RNGesus give the user XP.
  • Dying while on the Overdrive megastreak gives 4,000 XP.
  • If a player uses a Mark of Recon Essence, all other players in the lobby usually receive between 25 and 100 XP. Rarely, they may receive 1000 XP.
  • The Combo: XP mystic gives up to 36 XP per activation.

In addition to the base 5 XP on kill, there are multiple ways to increase the base XP earned on kill. The maximum XP earned on kill is 400, but can be increased in various ways.

  • The first 3 kills every life grant +4 XP.
  • The El Gato passive gives +5 XP for your first few kills every life, depending on the tier.
  • The Renown XP Bump upgrade gives up to +10 more base XP on kill.
  • The XP Bump mystic gives up to +6 base XP per kill.
  • The XP Stack killstreak increases XP earned on kill by a varying amount for your current prestige.
  • Killing players with a higher level than yourself earns bonus XP. This bonus XP is +1 XP for every 5 levels higher the victim is. Killing a "noob," or someone level 20 or lower, reduces XP gain by âˆ’10%.
  • Killing players while on a killstreak increases the base XP earned on kill depending on how high the player's streak is.
    • While on a 3 or 4 killstreak, gain +3 XP from the streak XP bonus.
    • While on a 5 to 19 killstreak, gain +5 XP from the streak XP bonus.
    • While on a 20 or higher killstreak, gain +3 XP per 10 kills in the streak, up to +30 XP at a 100 killstreak, or up to +60 XP at a 200 killstreak if the user has the Experience-industrial Complex upgrade.
  • The Streaker perk triples the killstreak bonus.
  • The Sweaty mystic increases the killstreak bonus XP by up to +60% and increases the maximum XP on kill by up to 100. Sweaty can be found on Mystic Swords, bows, standard colors of pants, and Rage Pants.
  • Killing a player who has a killstreak of higher than 3 rewards bonus XP as a "streak shutdown" bonus.
    • Shutting down a streak of 3 or 4 rewards +15 XP.
    • Shutting down a streak of 5 to 19 rewards +25 XP.
    • Shutting down a streak of 20 or higher rewards +15 XP per 10 kills in the streak, up to +300 XP (at a 200 killstreak shutdown).

The XP earned is then multiplied by all of the following that are applicable:

  • 2x Rewards, King of the Ladder, and Team Deathmatch increase XP earned from killing players by +100%. The King of the Hill event adds +300%. Being a beast in the Beast event grants +200% XP, but only on kills.
  • The Angel Faction's tier VII reward permanently increases XP earned by +1% XP for every time they have been claimed, up to +15% XP.
  • The Golden Helmet's Royalty enchantment gives +10% XP from kills and assists.
  • The Sewer Pants enchantment Worm increases XP gain from kills in the Sewers by +20%.
  • The Dark Pants enchantment Lycanthropy increases XP gain from kills at nighttime by +10%.
  • The Super Streaker killstreak gives the user 50 XP and permanently increases their XP gain by +5% (maximum of +50% XP) until they die or return to spawn. It triggers every 10 kills. Bonus XP earned from Super Streaker bypasses the per-kill maximum.
  • The XP Boost passive increases XP on kills by up to +50%, or +60% with the One-Upper renown upgrade.
  • The Pit MBA enchantment grants up to +30% XP if there are at least 5 players within 15 blocks of the player.
  • The XP Boost mystic enchantment increases XP on kills by up to +30%.
  • The Co-op Cat perk gives +50% XP on assists.
  • The Promotion!! renown upgrade upgrades the Assistant (to the) Streaker perk to give +15% XP on assists.
  • Certain megastreaks also give XP multipliers. Overdrive and Beastmode give +50% XP and Hermit gives +5% XP for every 10 kills, with a maximum of 200 kills (+75% XP).
  • The To the Moon megastreak gives a +20% XP multiplier and increases the maximum kill XP by +100. Additionally, the amount of XP earned while on a To the Moon streak is copied.
