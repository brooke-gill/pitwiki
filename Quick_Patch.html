Quick Patch
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Quick Patch was a minor update to the Hypixel Pit. It added new prestiges and some anti-abuse fixes.

New features added:

  • Added Prestiges XXXI to XXXV, all of which require 6,660,950 XP to complete.

Balance changes:

  • (↓) All Dark Pants enchantments now require at least one tier of Heresy to use.

Gameplay changes:

  • Arrows will now despawn 25 seconds after spawning, regardless of their current condition.
