Passives
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki

Passives are a category of upgrades. Passives are always equipped and reset when prestiging. Players can purchase and upgrade passives by clicking the Upgrades NPC at spawn. Upgrading passives costs gold.

The sixth tier of each passive upgrade requires One Upper, a renown upgrade unlocked at prestige XIV.
